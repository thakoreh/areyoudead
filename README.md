# Privacy Policy for Are You Dead?

Last updated: January 13, 2026

## Information We Collect

- Your name
- Emergency contact name and email
- Check-in timestamps
- Streak data

## How We Use Your Information

- To send alerts to your emergency contact
- To track your check-in streak
- To provide the service functionality

## Data Storage

- Data stored locally on your device via Chrome Storage
- Check-in data synced to our secure database (Supabase)
- Email alerts sent via Resend email service

## Data Sharing

We only share your data with:
- Your designated emergency contact (when you miss check-ins)
- Required service providers (Supabase for database, Resend for email)

We do NOT:
- Sell your data
- Share with advertisers
- Track your browsing

## Your Rights

You can:
- Update your emergency contact anytime
- Delete all data by resetting in Settings
- Export your data on request

## Contact

Questions? Email: noreply@alerts.neuralstackly.com
